# Brand Identity — Luxury Dental Paradiso

## Brand Essence

**Where Swiss precision meets natural beauty.**

We are a premium dental and facial aesthetics clinic that operates at the intersection of clinical excellence and artistic vision. Our brand communicates trust, luxury, and transformation — not through excess, but through restraint and mastery.

---

## Brand Positioning

| Axis | Position |
|---|---|
| Price | Ultra-premium (₊₊₊) |
| Style | Editorial / Swiss minimal |
| Tone | Warm authority |
| Audience | Affluent professionals, 28–55 |
| Competitors | Traditional dental brands (more clinical), med-spa brands (less precise) |

We own the intersection. Clinical precision *and* aesthetic beauty. Neither is compromised.

---

## Logo

**Mark**: Circle + "LD" in Playfair Display Bold, white on gold.
**Wordmark**: "Luxury Dental" in Playfair Display Semibold + "& Facial Estética" in small-caps tracking.

**Usage rules**:
- Minimum size: 32px diameter for the circle mark
- Clear space: equal to the circle diameter on all sides
- Never stretch, recolour, or use on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast

---

## Colour Story

**Gold** (`#C8A96B`) — The signature. Warmth, precision, exclusivity. Used sparingly as an accent.

**Olive** (`#556B2F`) — Nature, health, longevity. The brand's soul colour. Used in CTAs and key moments.

**Beige** (`#E8DCCB`) — Swiss linen. Calm, tasteful, European. Section backgrounds, breathing space.

**Black** (`#111111`) — Authority. Never cold — always paired with warm accents.

---

## Typography Voice

- **Playfair Display**: For moments of beauty, headline statements, brand voice. Use italic for emotional emphasis.
- **Inter**: For information, navigation, forms, data. Clean, modern, invisible.

**Never use Playfair Display below 16px.** It is a headline font.

---

## Tone of Voice

| Attribute | Expression |
|---|---|
| Confident | "We craft personalized treatments" (not "we try to") |
| Warm | "Begin your journey" (not "start your appointment") |
| Precise | Specific numbers: "1,200+ smiles", "15 years" |
| Minimal | Short sentences. No filler words. No superlatives without evidence. |
| Elevated | "Swiss precision", "artistic excellence" — not "great results" |

**Words we use**: Transform, Craft, Precision, Artistry, Natural, Excellence, Journey
**Words we avoid**: Cheap, Deal, Fast, Easy, Best (unsubstantiated), Amazing

---

## Photography Direction (Future)

When real photography is added:
- **Lighting**: Soft, natural, slightly warm. Never harsh clinical white.
- **Backgrounds**: Neutral — white, stone, linen textures.
- **Subjects**: Candid confidence, not forced smiles. Natural expressions.
- **Colour grade**: Warm, slightly desaturated, editorial.
- **Composition**: Generous negative space. Off-centre subjects.
